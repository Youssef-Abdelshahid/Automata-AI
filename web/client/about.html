About Automata AI

Automata AI is a revolutionary platform designed to democratize embedded machine learning by automating the entire model development lifecycle for edge devices.

Our Mission

We believe that powerful AI should run everywhere, from the smallest microcontrollers to industrial IoT devices. Our mission is to eliminate the complexity and expertise barriers that prevent developers from deploying AI on resource-constrained hardware.

The Problem We Solve

Developing AI models for embedded systems traditionally requires deep expertise in machine learning, hardware optimization, and embedded systems programming. Engineers face numerous challenges including model size constraints, limited computational resources, power consumption requirements, and the need for specialized knowledge in quantization, pruning, and hardware-specific optimizations.

Our Solution

Automata AI provides an end-to-end automated platform that takes your dataset and hardware specifications, then generates optimized, production-ready models. Our intelligent system handles model architecture selection, training, quantization, optimization, and packaging automatically.

Technology

Our platform leverages advanced neural architecture search, automated hyperparameter optimization, hardware-aware model compression techniques, and multi-objective optimization to balance accuracy, size, and performance. We support a wide range of hardware platforms from ultra-low-power microcontrollers to powerful single-board computers.

Who We Serve

Automata AI serves IoT developers, embedded systems engineers, product teams building smart devices, researchers prototyping edge AI applications, and companies deploying AI at scale across distributed devices.
